Project: Spatial Computing for Medical Simulation Training in XR Devices



At Jump Simulation Center, I took part in an ambitious project that aimed to revolutionize medical training through the integration of spatial computing technologies with Extended Reality (XR) devices. The goal was to create highly immersive, interactive medical environments that replicate real-world scenarios, providing medical students and professionals with hands-on experience like never before.

To bring this vision to life, I developed the backend infrastructure using Node.js and Express.js, which enabled real-time interactions between trainees and the AI-powered chatbot. Powered by Natural Language Processing (NLP) and OpenAI's GPT-4 model, the chatbot played a crucial role by offering guidance throughout simulated medical procedures. It answered complex questions and provided key insights during critical moments. A big part of my role was fine-tuning the chatbot's responses by connecting it to a specialized medical database, ensuring accuracy and relevance during the simulations.

This project wasn't just about guiding users—it was about completely transforming the training experience. I worked closely with a team of healthcare professionals to design interactive 3D environments that were synchronized with the AI chatbot, allowing trainees to manipulate virtual medical tools and practice procedures in real-time. These XR-powered simulations offered a seamless learning experience, immersing trainees in realistic medical scenarios where they could experience the same pressure and precision as they would in real-life situations.

Another key aspect of the project was training a specialized Large Language Model (LLM) for medical purposes. This allowed the AI assistant to provide contextually relevant, adaptive responses during simulations, enhancing the overall depth and realism of the training. I worked closely with healthcare experts to continuously refine the system, ensuring that it met the stringent standards required for medical training.

From backend development to real-time AI integration, this project utilized a wide range of technical skills. I used Python to train the LLM, and Node.js and Express.js to build the chatbot infrastructure. Additionally, I employed XR technologies to develop the interactive simulations. This project not only pushed the boundaries of AI in medical education but also demonstrated how advanced technology can revolutionize training in complex fields like healthcare.


Technical Skills Used:
